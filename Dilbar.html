DILBAR



INTRODUCTION

  • Dilbar is a super-yacht launched on 14 November 2015 at the German Lürssen shipyard anddelivered in 2016.
  • The interior design of Dilbar was designed by Andrew Winch and the exterior by Espen Oeino.
  • As of 2022, Dilbar is the sixth longest yacht in the world.She was built as Project Omar.
  • At 15,917 gross tonnage (GT), she is the third largest yacht by volume, after Fulk Al Salamah and REV Ocean.
