ECLIPSE



INTRODUCTION

  • The motor yacht Eclipse is the largest and most expensive private superyacht in the world.
  • It is owned by the Russian businessman and owner of Chelsea Football Club Roman Abramovich.
  • The yacht is estimated to have cost about €800m. It was delivered to the owner in December 2010.
  • The yacht accommodates three helicopters and a three-man private submarine, which can drive to a depth of 160ft.
