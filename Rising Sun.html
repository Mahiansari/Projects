RISING SUN



INTRODUCTION

  • Rising Sun is a motor yacht designed by Jon Bannenberg, and built in 2004 by Germany's Lürssen.
  • Rising Sun has been owned since 2010 by businessman David Geffen, who had bought a half share of the yacht initially in late 2006.
  • The 138.6m/454'9" motor yacht 'Rising Sun' was built by Lurssen in Germany at their Bremen shipyard.
  • Her interior is styled by design house Seccombe Design and she was delivered to her owner in June 2004.
