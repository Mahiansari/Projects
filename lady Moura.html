LADY MOURA




INTRODUCTION

  • Lady Moura is a private luxury yacht. She was the ninth largest private yacht
  • she was launched in 1990 for USD$200 million but has moved down the list in 2021 to number 48.
  • She was owned by Saudi Arabian businessman, Nasser Al-Rashid but bought by a Mexican businessman in 2021
  • Lady Moura has hosted several notable personalities, including George H. W. Bush and his wife Barbara.
