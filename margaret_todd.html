Margaret Todd



INTRODUCTION

  • Margaret Todd is a four-masted schooner sailing out of Bar Harbor, Maine.
  • The Margaret Todd carries nine sails on her four masts. The large sails are called, from front to back, the foresail, mainsail, mizzensail, and spankersail.
  • the Margaret Todd was built over a two year period by the Schreiber Boatyard in St. Augustine, Florida,
