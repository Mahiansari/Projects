Optimist



INTRODUCTION

  • The Optimist is a small, single-handed sailing dinghy intended for use by young people up to the age of 15.
  • The Optimist is one of the two most popular sailing dinghies in the world.
  • It is sailed in over 120 countries and it is one of only two sailboats as an International Class by World Sailing exclusively for sailors under 16.
