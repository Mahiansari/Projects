Tasar



INTRODUCTION

  • The Tasar is a 14.83-foot (4.52 m) fiberglass 2 person sailing dinghy with a mainsail and jib.
  • The boat was technologically advanced for its time and continues to evolve.
  • The design allows both the jib and gennaker to be used at the same time for power reaching.

HISTORY

Name Tasar
Designer Frank Bethwaite, Ian Bruce
Launched 1975
Crew 2
Design One-design

GENERAL CHARCTERISTICS

Type Monohull
Hull weight 149 lb (68 kg) (fully rigged, minus sails)
Beam 5 ft 9 in (1.75 m)
