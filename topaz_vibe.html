Topaz Vibe



INTRODUCTION

  • The Topaz Vibe is a British sailing dinghy.
  • There are two models, the Vibe and the Vibe X.
  • The Vibe is a World Sailing Learn to Sail class.

HISTORY

Name Topaz Vibe
Designer Ian Howlett & Rob White
Launched 2006
Crew 1-3
Trapeze Yes

GENERAL CHARCTERISTICS

Type Monohull
Contruction Topaz TRILAM
